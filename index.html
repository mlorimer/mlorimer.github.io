Marta Lorimer

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the University of Exeter and a Guest Teacher at the London School of Economics. I obtained my PhD from the European Institute, London School of Economics and Political Science.

Prior to joining the University of Exeter, I have held visiting positions at Forum MIDEM at TU Dresden and at the Centre d’Etudes Européennes in Sciences Po Paris.

My research has been published in the Journal of European Public Policy and in Ethnic and Racial Studies.

I have also published a chapter called ‘“Ni droite, Ni gauche, Français!” Far right populism and the future of Left/Right politics’ in the edited volume ‘Trumping the Mainstream: The conquest of mainstream democratic politics by far-right populism.’

I am a regular contributor to the LSE Europp blog and have written for OpenDemocracy, EuVisions, the LSE Brexit Blog and the CARR Insights blog. I tweet at @_mlorimer.

You can find out more about my research interests in the ‘About’ section.
